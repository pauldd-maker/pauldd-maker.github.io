My Autobiography

by Cameron Paul Seguisabal

About Me

Hello! My name is Cameron Paul Seguisabal, and I’m from the Philippines. I’m a student who enjoys learning about computers, technology, and design. I believe that every small step in learning helps me grow into a better version of myself.

My School Life

I’m currently studying in the field of Information Technology (IT). In school, I learn about programming, computer systems, and how technology shapes our daily lives. I enjoy working on projects, coding, and exploring new software that helps improve how we learn and communicate.

School has taught me discipline, teamwork, and problem-solving. My teachers and classmates inspire me to keep improving and to always stay curious about technology.

My Interests

Outside of class, I like to spend time learning new things about coding and game design — especially creating Roblox scripts and simple websites. I also enjoy playing games, exploring creative ideas, and hanging out with friends.

Future Goals

My goal is to become a skilled IT professional or developer. I want to create projects that can make people’s lives easier and inspire others to pursue technology as well. I believe that with passion and hard work, anything is possible.
